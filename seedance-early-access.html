🔓 What is Seedance Early Access?

ByteDance's Seedance Early Access Program allows select users to test experimental features before public release, including:

  • 🎬 Seedance 4.0 Beta - 4K resolution and 60-second videos
  • 🆕 Unreleased Features - Camera control, style transfer, AI actors
  • ⚡ Priority Access - Faster generation queues
  • 💬 Direct Feedback - Influence product development
  • 📊 Beta Pricing - Discounted rates for early adopters

Early Access is invite-only but anyone can join the waitlist.

❓ Early Access FAQ

How long does waitlist approval take?
Typically 2-4 weeks. Heavy users get approved faster. Check your email and Dreamina notification bell.
Can I lose early access?
Yes. Inactive users or those who abuse features may be removed from the program. Stay engaged and follow guidelines.
Is early access free?
No. Seedance 4.0 Beta costs $99/month (discounted from future $149/mo price). Free trial credits not included.
What happens when 4.0 goes public?
Early access users keep their discounted pricing ($99/mo) permanently as a thank you for testing.
Can I downgrade to 2.0?
Yes, anytime. Your 2.0 subscription and credits remain separate from beta access.
