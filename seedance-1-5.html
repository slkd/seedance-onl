Seedance 1.5: The Balanced Choice

Seedance AI 1.5 offers excellent quality at lower credit cost. Perfect for learning and iteration before upgrading to Seedance 2.0.

⚖️ Why Seedance 1.5?

Seedance 1.5 represents the sweet spot between cost and quality. It's 40% cheaper than v2.0 while delivering 80% of the quality.

Key Improvements Over 1.0

  • ✅ 1080p resolution (vs 720p in 1.0)
  • ✅ Up to 10-second videos (vs 5s)
  • ✅ Better character consistency
  • ✅ Improved physics and motion
  • ✅ Basic audio support

🎯 Best Use Cases for Seedance 1.5

✅ Perfect For:

  • Learning & Experimentation: Try prompts without burning credits
  • Draft Versions: Create multiple variations before final render
  • Social Media: 1080p is sufficient for Instagram/TikTok
  • High-Volume Creators: Produce more videos with same credit budget

⚠️ Upgrade to 2.0 For:

  • Professional client work requiring 2K
  • Projects needing advanced audio sync
  • Maximum character consistency
  • Cutting-edge quality
