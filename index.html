What is Seedance 2.0?

Seedance 2.0 is a groundbreaking multi-modal AI video generation model developed by ByteDance, the parent company of TikTok. It represents the cutting edge of AI video technology, available through the Dreamina platform.

Unlike traditional AI video tools that rely solely on text prompts, Seedance 2.0 (also known as Seedance AI, Seedance 2, or Seedance v2) allows users to combine multiple input types:

  • ✅ Up to 9 images for visual reference
  • ✅ Up to 3 video clips (15 seconds total) for motion reference
  • ✅ Up to 3 audio files for soundtrack and lip-sync
  • ✅ Text prompts for detailed scene descriptions
  • ✅ 12 total clips combined in a single project

This makes Seedance 2.0 the most versatile AI video generator on the market, surpassing competitors like OpenAI Sora 2 and Google Veo 3.1 in practical testing.

⚡ How Seedance 2.0 Works

Seedance 2.0 uses an innovative "@ mention" system that gives you precise control over how each uploaded asset contributes to your video generation:

1. Upload Your Assets

Add reference materials to your project:

  • Images: Character designs, scene references, style guides (up to 9)
  • Videos: Motion references, camera movement examples (up to 3, 15s total)
  • Audio: Background music, dialogue tracks, sound effects (up to 3)

2. Write Your Prompt

Use natural language to describe your video, explicitly referencing uploaded assets with @ mentions: 

Example: "Create a cinematic shot of @character_image walking through @city_video 
with @dramatic_music. Use the camera movement from @reference_clip."

3. Generate Your Video

Seedance 2.0 processes your inputs through ByteDance's advanced neural networks to create a coherent, high-quality video that respects all your creative constraints.

4. Refine and Export

  • Extend videos smoothly
  • Edit specific segments
  • Merge multiple clips
  • Export in 2K resolution, completely watermark-free

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Seedance 2.0 really better than Sora?
Yes. In practical testing, Seedance 2.0 demonstrates superior motion consistency, better audio integration, and more precise instruction following than OpenAI's Sora 2. The multi-modal input system gives creators far more control over the final output.
How much does Seedance 2.0 cost?
Seedance 2.0 operates on a credit-based system through Dreamina. New users receive free trial credits. Paid plans start at competitive rates with different credit allocations. See our detailed pricing guide for current rates.
Can I use Seedance 2.0 for commercial projects?
Yes, Seedance 2.0 supports commercial use with appropriate subscription plans. All exported videos are watermark-free, making them ready for professional projects.
What's the difference between Seedance 1.0, 1.5, and 2.0?
Each version represents a major improvement. Seedance 2.0 is 30% faster than 1.5, offers better consistency, improved audio features, and enhanced multi-modal capabilities. We recommend using 2.0 for the best results. Learn more about version differences.
Is there a Seedance 2.0 API?
Yes! ByteDance offers a Seedance API for developers who want to integrate video generation into their own applications. API access requires separate subscription and developer approval.
