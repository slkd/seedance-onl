Seedance 1.0: The Original AI Video Model

ByteDance's first AI video generation model. Learn about the legacy Seedance 1.0 and how it compares to Seedance 2.0.

📜 What is Seedance 1.0?

Seedance 1.0 was ByteDance's initial foray into AI video generation. Released as part of Dreamina platform, it demonstrated the potential of multi-modal video AI but had significant limitations.

Key Features

  • ✅ Text-to-video generation
  • ✅ Basic image reference support
  • ⚠️ Max resolution: 720p
  • ⚠️ Limited to 5-second videos
  • ❌ No audio synchronization
  • ❌ Inconsistent character preservation

Recommendation: Use Seedance 2.0 for all new projects. 1.0 is only available for compatibility.

🤔 When to Use Seedance 1.0?

✅ Good For:

  • Quick tests and experiments (very cheap credits)
  • Learning AI video basics without spending much
  • Historical/archival purposes

❌ Not Recommended For:

  • Any professional or production work
  • Social media content (resolution too low)
  • Projects requiring consistency

📚 Upgrade to Newer Versions

  • Seedance 1.5 - Recommended for Learning
  • Seedance 2.0 - Best for Production
  • Seedance 4.0 - Beta Experimental
