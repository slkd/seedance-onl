💰 Seedance 2.0 Pricing Overview

Seedance 2.0 uses a credit-based pricing system through ByteDance's Dreamina platform. Unlike subscription-only models, this gives you flexibility to pay for what you use.

How Seedance Credits Work

  • Each video generation costs a certain number of credits
  • Credit cost depends on video length, resolution, and complexity
  • Credits never expire once purchased
  • Free trial credits available for new users

❓ Pricing FAQs

Do Seedance credits expire?
No. Once you purchase credits, they remain in your account indefinitely. However, monthly subscription credits must be used within the billing period.
Can I buy additional credits?
Yes. All plans allow purchasing additional credit packs if you run out during a month.
Is there a Seedance API pricing?
Yes. The Seedance API has separate pricing starting at $99/month with API-specific credit allocations.
