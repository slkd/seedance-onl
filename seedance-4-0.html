Seedance 4.0: Next-Gen AI Video (Beta)

Experimental features pushing the boundaries of AI video generation. Limited beta access.

🚀 What is Seedance 4.0?

Seedance 4.0 is ByteDance's experimental next-generation video model, currently in limited beta. It explores cutting-edge features not yet ready for public release.

⚠️ Beta Status: Seedance 4.0 is not publicly available. Access requires invitation from ByteDance research team.

✨ Experimental Features

Reported Capabilities (from beta testers)

  • 🎬 4K Resolution Output: Double the quality of v2.0
  • ⏱️ Extended Duration: Up to 60-second continuous videos
  • 🤖 AI Director Mode: Automatic shot composition and editing
  • 🎨 Real-time Style Transfer: Apply artistic styles on the fly
  • 🔊 Advanced Audio: Multi-track audio with spatial sound
  • 🧠 Context Memory: Remember characters across multiple generations
