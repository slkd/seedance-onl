Seedance + CapCut Workflow

Combine Seedance AI generation with CapCut editing for professional video production.

🎬 Why Use Seedance with CapCut?

Both tools are from ByteDance, offering seamless integration:

  • ✨ Generate base videos in Seedance
  • ✂️ Edit, add effects, and refine in CapCut
  • 🎨 Access CapCut's extensive template library
  • 📱 Export optimized for social media platforms

🔄 Complete Seedance → CapCut Workflow

Step 1: Generate in Seedance

Create your AI video using Seedance 2.0, export in 2K resolution.

Step 2: Import to CapCut

Import Seedance video directly from Dreamina to CapCut (same ByteDance account).

Step 3: Edit & Enhance

  • Add transitions between multiple Seedance clips
  • Apply color grading and filters
  • Add text overlays and captions
  • Insert sound effects and music

Step 4: Export for Platform

Use CapCut's optimized export presets for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Generate in segments: Create 10-15s clips in Seedance, stitch in CapCut
  • Use CapCut templates: Apply trending templates to Seedance output
  • AI voice-over: Add CapCut's AI voice to Seedance silent videos
  • Auto-captions: CapCut can generate captions for Seedance audio
