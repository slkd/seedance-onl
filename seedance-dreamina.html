Seedance on Dreamina Platform

Access Seedance 2.0 through ByteDance's comprehensive Dreamina platform - the all-in-one AI creation suite.

🌟 What is Dreamina?

Dreamina is ByteDance's AI-powered creative platform that houses multiple AI generation models, including Seedance 2.0. Think of it as ByteDance's answer to Adobe Creative Cloud - but powered by AI.

Dreamina Platform Capabilities

  • ✨ AI Video Generation - Seedance 1.0, 1.5, 2.0, and 4.0
  • 🎨 AI Image Generation - High-quality image creation
  • ✂️ Video Editing - Built-in editor with AI enhancements
  • 🎵 Audio Tools - AI music and sound effect generation
  • 🔄 Format Conversion - Convert between media types

🚀 How to Access Seedance on Dreamina

Step 1: Create Dreamina Account

Sign up at the official Dreamina website using:

  • Email address
  • Google account
  • ByteDance/TikTok account

Step 2: Email Verification

Verify your email to activate your account and receive free trial credits.

Step 3: Navigate to Video Generation

From the Dreamina dashboard:

  1. Click "Video Generation" in the left sidebar
  2. Select "Seedance" from model options
  3. Choose version (1.0, 1.5, or 2.0)

Step 4: Start Creating

You'll have 50 free credits to explore Seedance's capabilities.

🔍 Dreamina vs Seedance vs Doubao

Confusion alert: ByteDance has multiple AI platforms:

  • Dreamina: Creative suite with Seedance, image gen, editing tools
  • Standalone Seedance: Doesn't exist - only via Dreamina
  • Doubao (豆包): Separate ByteDance AI assistant (like ChatGPT)

For video generation, use Dreamina.
