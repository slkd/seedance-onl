Seedance Image to Video Guide

Transform static images into dynamic AI videos with Seedance 2.0. Create professional animations from photos in seconds.

🎬 How Image-to-Video Works

Seedance 2.0 can animate still images by:

  1. Analyzing the image: Understanding subjects, depth, composition
  2. Adding motion: Creating natural movement based on your prompt
  3. Maintaining consistency: Keeping the original look and style
  4. Rendering in 2K: High-quality output at professional resolution

✨ Example Use Cases

Portrait Animation

Input: Headshot photo
Prompt: "Person smiling and nodding"
Result: Natural facial animation

Landscape Movement

Input: Mountain scene
Prompt: "Add gentle wind to trees, clouds moving"
Result: Living landscape

Product Demo

Input: Product photo
Prompt: "Slow 360° rotation"
Result: Professional showcase

📝 Step-by-Step Tutorial

Method 1: Simple Image Animation

1. Upload your image to Dreamina
2. Select "Image to Video" mode in Seedance 2.0
3. Write motion prompt: "Camera slowly zooms in"
4. Set duration: 5-10 seconds
5. Generate!

Method 2: Multi-Image Sequence

1. Upload 2-9 images
2. Prompt: "@image1 transitions smoothly to @image2"
3. Seedance creates morphing video between images

💡 Best Practices

  • High-resolution input: Use at least 1080p source images
  • Clear subjects: Well-lit, in-focus photos work best
  • Realistic motion: Request subtle movements for best results
  • Match style: Prompt motion that fits the image style
