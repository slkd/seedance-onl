ByteDance & Seedance: Behind the AI Revolution

How ByteDance, the company behind TikTok, created Seedance - the world's most advanced multi-modal AI video generator.

🏢 About ByteDance

ByteDance is a Chinese technology company founded in 2012, best known as the parent company of TikTok. With over 150,000 employees worldwide, ByteDance is a global leader in AI-powered content platforms.

ByteDance's AI Portfolio

  • 🎥 TikTok/Douyin: AI-powered short video platform
  • 🤖 Doubao (豆包): Conversational AI assistant
  • ✨ Dreamina: AI creation suite (includes Seedance)
  • 🎨 Capcut: Video editing with AI features
  • 📰 Toutiao: AI news recommendation

🚀 Seedance Development History

2023: Seedance 1.0 Launch

ByteDance enters AI video generation market with experimental model.

2024: Rapid Iteration

Seedance 1.5 demonstrates ByteDance's commitment to quality improvements.

2025-2026: Seedance 2.0 Era

Multi-modal capabilities and 2K output establish Seedance as industry leader, surpassing OpenAI Sora and Google Veo.
